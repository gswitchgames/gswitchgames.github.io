Description

The game you've been waiting for has finally arrived with the third episode! In the third chapter, G-Switch introduces you to a number of new things. In the single player game mode, you will now have to handle two runners. Furthermore, you should be cautious of any sawblade-encrusted paths. You will employ two different boosters and cloning in multiplayer game mode. This chapter introduces five new characters. With the multiplayer game mode option, you may play with up to eight people and select their controls. The player who goes the furthest wins the game.

Controls:

Player 1: "LEFT-CLICK"
Player 2-8: Define it from the game menu.

Tips and tricks:

  • You can play multiplayer with real people or AIs.
  • Try to switch just before an obstacle rather than when you hit it.
  • You can use the mouse or keyboard to play. Find what works for you!

About the creator:

G-Switch 3 is created by Serius Games working in Portugal.
